About Our Agri-Sacco

We are a community-based Savings and Credit Cooperative dedicated to empowering farmers through financial inclusion and sustainable agricultural practices.

Our Mission

To provide affordable financial services to smallholder farmers and connect them to sustainable markets while promoting environmentally friendly farming practices.

Our Vision

To create a thriving agricultural ecosystem where farmers have access to resources, knowledge, and markets to build sustainable livelihoods and communities.

Our Values

Community cooperation, environmental sustainability, financial inclusion, innovation, transparency, and empowerment of smallholder farmers.

Our Services

We offer a range of financial and agricultural support services designed to help our members thrive.

Savings Solutions

Secure and flexible savings options including goal-based savings programs specifically designed for agricultural needs.

Agricultural Loans

Tailored loan products for seeds, equipment, fertilizers and other farming needs with flexible repayment schedules aligned with harvest cycles.

Marketplace Access

Connect directly with buyers and sellers in our marketplace platform to get better prices and reduce middlemen.

Resource Sharing

Access to shared agricultural tools, machinery, and land through our collaborative resource sharing platform.

Community Forum

Share knowledge, ask questions, and learn from other farmers and agricultural experts in our community forum.

Expert Consultations

Book consultations with agricultural experts for personalized advice on farming techniques, crop selection, and pest management.
